About Us

The modern version of the KCFGC actually originated a little over a decade ago. After the "dark ages" (the period of time when fighting game releases had come to a crawl before Street Fighter IV's launch') a fighting game scene had developed to put a little fire into the "old school" players located in KC. This small scene was in Oak Grove Missouri and was being helmed by Robbie Votaw, who had stumbled upon a new local card shop called Pew Pew Card and Games. It was here that he would build his local scene from next to nothing with the help of KC's head tournament organizer Jonathan Spotswood and breed fighting game players to rival the long time players of Kansas City. All of that happened well over 10 years ago and now the two scenes have assimilated and evolved into a welcoming community geared towards raising everyone up and competing on the national level. "The worst thing you can do when it comes to fighting games is never pick up a controller" - Robbie Votaw

Why Choose Us

We are the central hub for all things fighting games within the greater Kansas City area. Offering not only places and events for competitve players to put themselves to the test against the best in the area, but also a place where new players can come to learn and be mentored so that they too can join the ranks of the competitive players in the open bracket format. With a wide variety of games to be played and lessons to be learned we are confident that anyone can find their place within the KCFGC.

Competition

With a long history of tournaments ranging from 30 man locals at Top in the Shop, to 200 man regionals at Kansas City's premiere Fighting Game Tournament MAXOUT, you can find anything to fit your competition level. The local level is where most players get their starts, almost every current top local player got their start from going to their first Top in the Shop.
