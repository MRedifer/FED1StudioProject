Class Subjects

Street Fighter V

Classic head to head fighting game action! Players of all skill levels can learn from our Street Fighter V courses, from motion inputs, combos, and spacing to more advanced techniques and philosophies like footsies, mind games, and conditioning. Step back into the roots of fighting games with Street Fighter V

Courses

Guilty Gear Strive

Heaven or Hell? Duel 1, Lets Rock! The latest entry in the Guilty Gear series is a bit of a departure from the previous games but that hasn't stopped our instructors from becoming experts at its new mechanics. If you like fast paced, high damage fighting backed by a heavy metal soundtrack you should probably look into our courses for Guilty Gear Strive.

Courses

Tekken 7

Power is everything, and the best fights are personal! If the realm of 3d fighting seems more your style we have globally recognized Tekken players ready to take you under their wing. From side stepping to korean back dashing and electrics, our instructors can do it all. A wide selection of characters including guests from other media all with DEEP move lists can be yours to master if you choose a Tekken 7 course.

Courses

Ultimate Marvel vs Capcom 3

You wanna learn how to do an infinite? The hypest cross over to ever grace a fighting game stage with rivalries that span decades, Marvel is considered the pinnacle of VS fighting games. With a cast of instantly recognizable characters and easy to pick up but hard to master mechanics and moves, our instructors are more than capable of guiding you to your first infinite. You have to exercise caution though, a single touch could spell disaster for one of your three characters!

Courses

Dragon Ball Fighterz

Do you think you are the strongest under the heavens? Fast paced VS gameplay with the one of the most popular media franchises in the world? We don't have to tell you anymore, our instructors will have you comboing people into outerspace and turning Super Saiyan in no time. Similar to other VS games you have 3 characters on your team with tag ins and supers galore. What's your power level? Take a DBFZ course and find out!

Courses

SkullGirls

This game has some real staying power! It's first version release was all the way back in 2012 and the 2nd Encore version is STILL being played to this day! Gameplay similar to the other VS games but with a hand drawn anime art style and unique character designs set this game apart from the rest. Our instructor is a mutliple time top 8 finisher at several major world level tournaments and he couldn't be more ready to teach you your first Dual Hyper Combo.
