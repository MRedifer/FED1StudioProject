Training and Instructors

The Mission

Within the KCFGC we have a wide variety of people interested in a wide variety of games. If you want long combos, multiple characters on your screen and flashy super moves you might be interested in trying a VS game like Dragon Ball FighterZ, Skullgirls, or Marvel vs Capcom. Prefer old school 2d style action? Street Fighter is the largest sub community within the KCFGC with tons of players ready and willing to teach, Mortal Kombat, Granblue Fantasy VS, and Samurai Shodown are just a few other in that 2d realm. If you prefer your fighting in a 3d environment our Tekken scene has nationally recognized players and old heads that have been playing fighting games longer than some of the people in the scene have been alive. Once you are vetted you just might get invited to the legendary Tekken house where the best players go to train. All you have to do is decide what game looks the most fun and start playing!
